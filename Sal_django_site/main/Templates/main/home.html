How It Works

1.


Sign up or login

Want post free food supply or needs on our map? Click the 'Sign up' tab in the top right to make an account.

If you just want view our map, there's no need to sign up.

2.


Go to the ‘My Posts’ page

This is where you can find and create posts about your need or supply of free food.

If you want to create your own posts for supply/demand of free food you DO need to make an account ;)

3.


Click a cross to create a post

Be sure to include as many details as possible, including the time and dates it will be available, whether they have to pick it up or it can be delivered.

Be sure to emphasize any foods that are commonly associated with allergies like nuts and shellfish.
