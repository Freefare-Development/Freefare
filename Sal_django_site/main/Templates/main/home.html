How It Works

You don’t need an account to find or give free food!

Go to the ‘Map’ page, search your local area, filter for ‘donor’ or ‘needs’, and reach out to organizations directly.

Posting food giveaways or requests

Advertise your leftovers in a post so that those who need what you have can find it. To post your surplus food donation or food request: 1. Sign up or login if you already have an account 2. Go to the ‘My Posts’ page 3. Click the respective cross to create a post describing the food you want to give away or pick up. You’re done! Check the map for your post and we’ll broadcast your surplus or need to our network.

There are lots of ways to help!

If you would rather give your time, we have tips on how you can help connect organizations with surplus to groups who could use it. Contact Us to learn more.
