How It Works

Psssssst!
(You don’t need an account unless you want to post.
Click the 'Map' page to simply find free food.)

1.

Sign up or login

Go to the ‘Map’ page, search your local area, filter for ‘donor’ or ‘needs’, and reach out to organizations directly.

2.

Go to the ‘My Posts’ page

This is where you can find and create your posts.

If you want to create your own posts for supply/demand of free food you DO need to make an account ;)

3.

Click a cross to create a post about a supply or need for free food.

If you would rather give your time, we have tips on how you can help connect organizations with surplus to groups who could use it. Contact Us to learn more.
